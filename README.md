🚀 HR Dashboard Overview

Step into a smart, data-driven view of your workforce with this interactive HR Dashboard—designed to turn raw employee data into meaningful insights at a glance.

🔢 Key Highlights

Right at the top, four powerful KPIs give you a quick snapshot of organizational health:

👥 1K Employees — your total workforce
💰 10M Monthly Income — overall earnings
📚 4K Trainings Completed — learning and development activity
⏱️ 97K Hourly Rate (Total) — combined workforce cost
🎛️ Interactive Filters

Dive deeper into your data with dynamic controls:

🎓 Education Level Slider (1–5)
💼 Job Role Selector (Healthcare, HR, Lab Technician, Manager, etc.)
⚧ Gender Filter (Male / Female)

These filters let you instantly customize the dashboard and uncover specific insights.

📊 Visual Insights

Explore workforce trends through intuitive visualizations:

📉 Hourly Rate by Gender — compare pay distribution across genders
💼 Monthly Income by Department — see which departments drive revenue
😊 Job Satisfaction by Role — understand employee sentiment
🎓 Education by Job Role — analyze qualifications across positions
